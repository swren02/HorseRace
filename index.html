How it Works

Description:

On the right, check off one of the cryptocurriencies that you want to place your bets on. Once you have it selected, click the enable ethereum button to connect to your Metamask walled. Once the permission is granted, you can specify how much ETH you want to bet and enter it in the field. Then click the send ethereum button to finalize your transaction. Metamask should open and you can confirm the details of your transaction. Then click on accept. You can also view the ceryptocurrency prices from the link below
